Ingredients

  • Ham, chorizo, salami, ... meat to grill
  • Potatoes
  • Raclette cheese
  • The Raclette aparatus

Steps

  1. Get your friends and/or family
  2. Plug in the raclette aparatus and let it heat up
  3. Boil the potatoes
  4. Slice the raclette cheese (if not pre-sliced) and let everyone put a slice in your skillet
  5. Shove the skillets in their respective slots in the aparatus and let the cheese melt (it should start to boil and pop when ready)
  6. As the cheese melts, grill a piece of your favored meat and grab a boiled potato
  7. When the cheese is ready, either lay it over the potato or next to it, and eat a combination of cheese-potato-grilled meat in one bite
  8. Get another slice of cheese and start again untill full
